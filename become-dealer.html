Dealer Requirements

Experience

No experience required in window treatment industry.

Location

It's helpfull to have store front or home office. No physical retail location required in your service area. Dealers can sell anywhere they wish.

Financial

No capital required to maintain inventory and operational expenses.dealers should have their own vehicle, measuring tapes, ladder, drill gun. To become Winlor dealer, one need to buy at least one fabric catalog and at least one finished blind sample.

Training

Dealers should be willing to learn. we will provide training on measuring, installing, marketing, placing orders, calculating prices, making and setting up website and anything else required to run shades business.
